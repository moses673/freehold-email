OPINION

The Problem with SaaS

You're on a treadmill.

SaaS companies make money through recurring revenue. Monthly subscriptions. Annual contracts. The business model depends on locking you in and raising prices incrementally.

Every year, prices go up.

Subscription email platforms pricing has increased 15-30% per year for the last five years. A tool that cost $20/month in 2020 now costs $50-100/month for the same features.

You can't escape.

Your subscriber data is locked inside their platform. Your workflows depend on their integrations. Migrating to a competitor costs time and money. You're trapped.
